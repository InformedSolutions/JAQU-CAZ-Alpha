Birmingham Clean Air Zone

Birmingham operates a Class A Clean Air Zone, meaning that buses, coaches, taxis and private hire vehicles are subject to a charge if they do not comply with the emission standards.

Check online to find out whether your vehicle complies with the emissions standards of the Birmingham Clean Air Zone.

You'll need your vehicle's registration number.
